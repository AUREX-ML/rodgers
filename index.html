About

Where electrical systems meet software intelligence.

I am an Electrical Systems Engineer passionate about technology, sustainable development, and innovation. My expertise lies at the intersection of electrical engineering, software development, IoT, and artificial intelligence, enabling me to bridge the gap between hardware and software systems.
